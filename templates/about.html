Secret Watch Club is a place where you can find new watches alongside those you already know well. The collection here has been curated with the members of the r/watches community in mind, but the tastes and sensibilities represented here are universal. If you have a watch you want added to the collection we would love to hear from you!

Each individual watch is represented by:
A high-resolution picture of its dial
Price (updated every 15 minutes with Amazon’s API)
Amazon link (with referral tag)
Price history link (camelcamelcamel)
r/watches search link (join the discussion!)

Here at Secret Watch Club we hope you will find what you’ve been looking for. Whether you fall in love with a dial after seeing it for the first time or find a piece you have been meaning to acquire, the price history alongside each watch will help you decide if now is a good time to buy. Should you decide to do that on Amazon, you can click on the cart next to any watch to find it there.

This website runs on the Google Cloud Platform. Source code available on GitHub.
